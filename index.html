Research interests

Exploring how spatial and temporal dynamics of simple self-assembling units create complex, adaptive systems that produce emergent phenomena like cognition and learning. I'm currently taking techniques from algorithmic self-assembly, molecular dynamics, stochastic simulations, and chemical reaction networks, together with neuroscience data on neuron function, topology, and brain connectivity, and blending them into machine learning frameworks.

Deeper dive on my blog.
